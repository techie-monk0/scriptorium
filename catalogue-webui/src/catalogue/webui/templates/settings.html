Inbox folders

New books are dropped here, then a Scan ingests them. Inbox folders are scanned first, so a fresh drop shows up in Review within seconds instead of after the whole library; on review, a book is filed OUT of here onto its subject shelf. This is separate from your library roots — list each inbox folder explicitly (a folder is an inbox only if it's listed here).
